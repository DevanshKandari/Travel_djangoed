Trekking

Many people dream of trekking in Uttarakhand, right in the alps of the mighty Himalayas. Uttarakhand is a hilly state to a great extent, located at the foothills of the Himalayan mountain ranges. The state is blessed with natural resources – rivers, forests and most importantly snow-clad mountains. The most revered Dhams in India – Char-dhams are Hindu temples of Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamunotri are nestled in these ferocious mountains. Trekking is a very popular sport in Uttarakhand due to its accessibility. From beginners to experts can test their stamina by trekking these mountains. You can find Treks in Uttarakhand from easy to difficult climb.

Some of the Famous Treks are :

  • Tungnath-Chandrashila Trek
  • Kedarnath Trek
  • Roopkund Trek
  • Rudranath Trek
  • Madhmaheswar Trek
  • Valley Of Flowers
