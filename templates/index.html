Frequently Asked Questions

It is best to visit Mussourie in between June and December. Because in June, Mussourie is probably the most crowded place in Uttarakhand. You can visit other hill-stations in Month of June to avoid the scorching sun of summer.

Uttarakhand is expensive state but the places which are considered as tourism hotspot, are much expensive due to their Ecstatical, Historical and Spiritual Significance. So If you want some place to visit under the budget then consider travelling in off season, unless you are going for Spiritual purpose as the temples are not always open they have their fixed time to visit in a year.

Uttarakhand is full of places where you find bugyal or snow , trekk or plane , Spiritual or Historical so i would say that whole Uttarakhand is must visit but i you ask me out of the places i visit to recommend Harshil Valley , Uttarkashi . You will not be disappointed.

If you are travelling in two wheeler,in the mountains, then avoid riding at night as the occurence of wild animals at night is more in Uttarakhand Mountain Areas.

Uttarakhand is known as 'Dev Bhoomi' or the 'Land of the Gods' due to its rich religious and spiritual heritage. The region is home to several ancient temples, pilgrimage sites, and shrines, which are believed to be associated with various gods and goddesses of Hindu mythology.

Weather is most important thing to know about in Uttarakhand due to past experiences. So have a look on weather forecast before travelling in Uttarkhand. As the Natural Disasters are common in Uttarakhand besides Natural beauties.
